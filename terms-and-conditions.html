1. Program Description

Progresso Insurance and Multiservices Inc. ("we," "us," or "our") operates an SMS text messaging program to communicate with existing policyholders. This program includes:

  • Personalized birthday greetings sent annually to customers on file
  • Account notifications related to policy status, renewals, or important updates
  • Service reminders and general customer care messages

These messages are sent only to customers who have provided their mobile phone number and consented to receive SMS communications from Progresso Insurance and Multiservices Inc. at the time of enrollment or policy signing.

2. Consent & Opt-In

By providing your mobile phone number to Progresso Insurance and Multiservices Inc. during policy enrollment or through a signed consent form, you agree to receive SMS text messages from us. Your consent is not a condition of purchasing any product or service.

3. Message Frequency

Message frequency varies depending on your account activity. You may receive approximately 1 to 4 messages per year. This includes your annual birthday greeting and any important account notifications.

4. Message & Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Contact your wireless carrier for details about your messaging plan and any applicable charges.

5. Opt-Out Instructions

You may opt out of our SMS program at any time. To stop receiving messages:

Reply STOP to any message you receive from us. You will receive a confirmation message and will be removed from our SMS list immediately.

You may also contact us directly to request removal from our SMS program.

6. Help & Support

If you need assistance with our SMS program:

Reply HELP to any message you receive from us, or contact our office directly for support.

7. Privacy & Data Use

Your mobile phone number and personal information will not be sold, shared, or disclosed to third parties for marketing purposes. We use your information solely to deliver the communications described in this program. For full details, please review our Privacy Policy.
